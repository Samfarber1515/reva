04The membership

Inside the membership.

Five departments running in the background of your life. Here's exactly what they do.

No. 01

Your health team

A small medical office working quietly on behalf of you and your family.

You'll see the right specialist this week, not in six months. The cardiologist, dermatologist, oncologist, or whoever you actually need — booked through our network of physicians who keep slots open for our members.
A doctor on the phone with you tonight, not Monday morning. When something unclear is happening at 11 PM — your kid's fever, a strange chest pain, a rash you don't recognize — we get a real physician on the line within the hour.
Your medical history, organized in one place and ready to send anywhere. Every test, scan, and prescription from every doctor you've ever seen — securely stored and sent to any physician in five minutes when you need it.
No. 02

Your financial office

The structure wealthy families have quietly used for decades — set up properly, kept current, and run on your behalf.

A senior CPA on retainer who actually plans your year, not just files it. Quarterly reviews, year-end strategy, structuring decisions made before they cost you — the kind of relationship most people don't realize is available until they have it.
An estate and trust attorney working in the background of your life. Wills, trusts, succession plans — drafted, reviewed, and updated as your situation changes. The documents are in place long before anyone needs them.
Every IRS letter answered without you opening the envelope. Audits, balance-due notices, penalty letters — they go directly to your CPA, get handled, and you hear about it only when there's a real decision to make.
No. 03

Your household

Every property you own, running the way you'd run it if you had time to run it.

The plumber, the electrician, the HVAC guy — at your door today. We've vetted the contractors in every city you live in. When something breaks, we make the call, schedule the visit, and meet them at the house if you need us to.
The cleaners, organizers, and handymen you'd trust with your keys. Background-checked, references verified, scheduled on a recurring cadence that fits your week. We pay them, manage them, and replace anyone who doesn't meet the bar.
The to-do list you've been ignoring since March, finally cleared. The warranty claim, the broken sprinkler, the missing window screen, the fence the neighbors keep mentioning — we work through the backlog one quiet line at a time.
No. 04

Your travel desk

A team that books every leg of every trip — and quietly fixes the ones that fall apart while you sleep.

Every flight, hotel, and car booked from a single text. Tell us where, when, and who's coming — we handle the routing, the seats, the room, the airport pickup. You walk through the terminal and everything's already arranged.
Cancelled flights and storms rerouted while you're still asleep. We monitor your itinerary. When JFK closes or the connection vanishes, we move you to the next viable option, get a new car to the right curb, and text you before your alarm goes off.
Trusted partners on the ground in over forty cities. The driver, the doctor, the dinner reservation, the local fixer who knows the city — already arranged in the cities you visit, ready before your plane lands.
No. 05

Your life desk

The hundred small things that fall through the cracks of a successful week — caught, sourced, and signed in your handwriting.

You'll never miss another anniversary, birthday, or milestone. We track the dates that matter for you, your spouse, your kids, your parents, your closest friends — and on the morning of, the dinner is booked, the gift is at the door, and the card is on your desk to sign.
The right gift, every time, without you walking into a store. From a first-edition book your father-in-law has wanted for years to a piece commissioned from a designer who doesn't take walk-ins — we source it, wrap it, write the note, and have it delivered the way you'd want it delivered.
And anything else you can think of — usually yes, occasionally no. Sushi flown in from Tokyo for a dinner party. A helicopter at sunrise for an anniversary. The interesting part isn't whether we can do it — it's how we get it done.

Five domains. One thread. Zero of it on your plate.

We're watching what's coming for you — the trip you'd love to take, the doctor you've been meaning to find, the storm that's about to cancel your flight — and we get ahead of it. Most weeks, you'll get a text from us before you would have thought to send one.

That's the whole difference. Reva moves before you do.

Three things, in order:

1. Means. Liquid net worth above $5M, or income above $1M annually. We verify quietly.

2. Fit. Referred by a current member, or interviewed by the founder. We keep the membership tight on purpose — a small team can only run a small number of lives well.

3. Posture. Comfortable with text as the only interface. Willing to be specific about what you want back. The members who get the most from Reva are the ones willing to hand things over completely.
